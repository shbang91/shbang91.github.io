--- layout: home author_profile: true classes: wide title: About --- I am a PhD Candidate in the Human Centered Robotics Lab at the University of Texas at Austin, advised by Professor Luis Sentis. My research interest lies broadly in the whole-body motion planning and control algorithms for deployable and versatile humanoid robots. I strongly believe that robust and efficient reactive and predictive whole-body control would enable human-level autonomy and agility for humanoid robots.
